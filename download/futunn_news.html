      热搜第一！瑞幸联名迪奥？官方回应…

      Hot search number one! Is Lucky co-branded with Dior? The official response...

      Securities Times ·  17:12

      瑞幸又上热搜！

      近日，随着瑞幸茅台酱香拿铁成为爆款，网传瑞幸准备与迪奥联名引发网友讨论。10月15日，瑞幸官方客服回应称，暂未接到相关通知，具体以官方消息为准。

      瑞幸与迪奥联名？官方客服回应

      今日，一则“#瑞幸要和dior联名了吗#”的词条登上微博热搜第一名，有微博大V发出了瑞幸×Dior联名的宣传图。

      随后，瑞幸官方客服就此进行了回应：“暂未接到相关通知，具体以官方消息为准。”

      公开资料显示，克里斯汀·迪奥（Christian Dior），简称迪奥（Dior或CD），是法国时尚消费品牌，隶属于酩悦·轩尼诗-路易·威登集团。迪奥主要经营男女手袋、女装、男装、男女鞋履、首饰、香水、化妆品、童装等高档消费品，是名副其实的奢侈品牌。

      “猫和老鼠”联名产品销售火爆

      在与茅台联名推出酱香拿铁爆红后，10月9日，瑞幸咖啡再发力，推出与经典动画IP“猫和老鼠”联名的产品——马斯卡彭生酪，一经推出，就销售火爆。瑞幸抖音直播间在线人数最高达一万多人，新品套餐一上线就被秒光，显示已售出50万份+，一度登上团购带货榜第一名。 

      而此次推出的新品，9.9元就能买到！不少网友直呼太香了，买下的满满都是童年的回忆。

      据瑞幸介绍，“马斯卡彭生酪拿铁”新品含丹麦进口马斯卡彭芝士，奶风味约提升24%，浓缩牛乳风味精华，奶味更饱满丰厚；并特定臻选100%阿拉比卡咖啡豆，一杯涵盖“芝士香”“咖啡香”“奶香”。 

      不仅如此，只要同一订单购买任意2杯饮品即送TOM &JERRY联名贴纸1份，联名周边共有4款杯套和3款纸袋，随机发送。对于点单杯的消费者们也充满惊喜，特别设置的单杯纸袋，让不少网友直呼：“单杯终于也可以有漂亮的袋子！” 

      《2022本土咖啡消费趋势洞察报告》数据显示，中国咖啡消费年均增速达15%。这意味着，中国咖啡市场发展步入“快车道”。在咖啡产品日益丰富、消费场景愈发多元的今天，如何更好占领消费者心智，是摆在每个咖啡品牌面前的核心问题。 

      作为咖啡界的联名狂人，瑞幸经常会推出一些让人眼前一亮的联名。据瑞幸2022年财报显示，2022年全年，瑞幸共推出了140款全新现制饮品，即使是以新品见长的茶饮，出新品的速度也完全赶不上瑞幸。

      瑞幸猎奇感十足的联名玩法层出不穷。1月，瑞幸与“福娃之父”韩美林联手推出春节限定“幸运兔much”；2月，瑞幸与线条小狗联名推出情人节特饮；4月，瑞幸与电影《哆啦A梦：大雄与天空的理想乡》联名推出礼品卡；6月，瑞幸联名镖人推出新品“昆仑煮雪拿铁”。 

      七夕期间，瑞幸与线条小狗“再续前缘”，推出9.9元“修狗黑凤梨”新品和爱情卡牌周边；8月，瑞幸联名维多利亚的秘密，推出桂花龙井；9月，瑞幸与茅台联名推出均价19元一杯的“酱香拿铁”，首日销量超542万杯，单日销售额超1亿，被餐饮行业热议为现象级营销事件。

      新式茶饮咖啡热衷联名出品 

      品牌联名往往是从全新角度将原先毫无交集的元素重构，为消费者重塑品牌新形象，亮出一种新态度，从而招揽新顾客。因此，品牌往往可以通过联名，借助其他品牌热度，为自身增添新的活力。 

      Quest Mobile发布的《2023夏日经济之现制咖啡和茶饮市场洞察》显示，2023年1月至7月，现制咖啡联名数量41个，现制茶饮联名数量62个。在各联名形式中，品牌联名类型超半数，成为最高频次合作形式。

      除了瑞幸以外，各种新式茶饮也热衷出联名款。其中，在10月3日霸王茶姬与盗墓笔记联名活动上线当天，联名产品之一“青青糯山”销量增长超130%。在中秋、国庆双节期间正值亚运会，沪上阿姨鲜果茶也借助体育赛事热潮，全新上市“多金黄桃”系列饮品，邀请冠军电竞选手一诺担任“鲜果茶体验官”，并推出联名周边、定制金牌抽奖、饮品券放送等丰富的营销活动。 

      9月14日，奈雪的茶联名薄盒范特西音乐宇宙重磅推出联名款奶茶。奈雪X范特西联名奶茶首日开售瞬间爆单，首日销售量高达146万杯，创造了奈雪新品首日销量的新纪录，同时也创下了单日门店销售新高。全国网友大呼周杰伦专辑“回忆杀”的同时，借机推出“周周9.9元喝奈雪鲜奶茶”也卖爆了。

      更早时候，今年5月，喜茶也推出了联名产品，其联合芬迪推出了相关套餐，一杯价格19元的喜茶联名套餐一度被炒到了上百元。 

      但归根结底，消费者最终关注的始终是产品本身，品牌安身立命的核心也是产品本身。联名只是一种手段，给品牌带来附加值，消费者的长期性购买最终还是要依靠产品品质，产品开发的创新加上营销带来的惊喜体验，才能有持久吸引力。

      Ruixing is in the hot search again!

On October 15, Ruixing's official customer service responded that no relevant notice had been received yet; the details are subject to official information.

      Is Lucky and Dior co-branded? Official customer service response

      Subsequently, Ruixing's official customer service responded: “No relevant notice has been received yet. The details are subject to official information.”

      According to public information, Christian Dior (Dior or CD) for short, is a French fashion consumer brand belonging to the Moet Hennessy-Louis Vuitton Group. Dior mainly sells high-end consumer goods such as men's and women's handbags, women's clothing, men's clothing, men's and women's shoes, jewelry, perfume, cosmetics, and children's clothing. It is a veritable luxury brand.

      Sales of the “cat and mouse” co-branded product are booming

      After the joint launch of the sauce-flavored latte with Maotai became popular, on October 9, Lucky Coffee made another effort to launch a product co-branded with the classic animated IP “Cat and Mouse” — mascarpone cheese. Once launched, sales were booming. The number of people online in the Ruixing Douyin live broadcast room reached more than 10,000 people. As soon as the new package was launched, it was revealed that 500,000 copies + had been sold. At one point, it ranked first in the group buying and delivery list.

      And the new product launched this time can be bought for 9.9 yuan! Many netizens bluntly called it so fragrant; all of their purchases were full of childhood memories.

      According to Ruixing, the new “mascarpone cheese latte” contains mascarpone cheese imported from Denmark. The flavor of milk has increased by about 24%, concentrating the essence of milk flavor, making the milk more full and rich; it also specially selected 100% Arabica coffee beans. One cup covers “cheese flavor,” “coffee flavor,” and “milk flavor.”

      Furthermore, as long as you buy any 2 drinks in the same order, you will receive 1 TOM & JERRY co-branded sticker. There are 4 cup holders and 3 paper bags around the joint brand, which are sent randomly. Consumers who ordered a single cup were also full of surprise. The specially designed single-cup paper bag made many netizens shout, “You can finally have a beautiful bag for a single cup!”

      According to data from the “2022 Local Coffee Consumption Trend Insight Report”, the average annual growth rate of coffee consumption in China has reached 15%. This means that the development of the Chinese coffee market is on the “fast track.” Today, when coffee products are increasingly abundant and consumption scenarios are becoming more and more diverse, how to better capture the minds of consumers is the core issue facing every coffee brand.

      As a co-celebrity freak in the coffee industry, Ruixing often launches some eye-catching co-names. According to Lucky's 2022 financial report, throughout 2022, Lucky launched a total of 140 new ready-made drinks. Even for teas that are known for being new, the speed at which new products are produced cannot keep up with Lucky at all.

      Lucky has endless co-branded gameplay that is full of sense of wonder. In January, Lucky and the “father of Fuwa” Han Meilin joined forces to launch the Spring Festival limited edition “Lucky Rabbit”; in February, Lucky and Line Puppy jointly launched a special Valentine's Day drink; in April, Lucky and the movie “Doraemon: Nobita and the Sky's Dream Land” jointly launched a gift card; in June, Lucky and its famous people launched the new product “Kunlun Cooking Snow Latte.”

      During Tanabata, Lucky and Line Puppy “renewed their relationship” and launched a new 9.9 yuan “Shu Gou Black Pineapple” product and love card accessories; in August, Lucky and Victoria's Secret launched Osmanthus Longjing; in September, Ruixing and Maotai jointly launched a “Sauce-flavored Latte” with an average price of 19 yuan per cup, with sales exceeding 5.42 million cups on the first day and sales exceeding 100 million dollars in a single day. It was hotly discussed as a phenomenal marketing event by the catering industry.

      Modern tea and coffee are passionate about co-production 

      Brand co-branding often reconstructs elements that did not previously intersect from a new perspective, reshapes a new brand image for consumers, and reveals a new attitude, thus attracting new customers. Therefore, brands can often add new vitality to themselves through joint names and take advantage of the popularity of other brands.

      According to the “2023 Summer Economy: Ready-Made Coffee and Tea Market Insights” published by Quest Mobile, from January to July 2023, there were 41 co-brands of freshly made coffee and 62 co-brands of freshly made tea. Among the various forms of joint branding, more than half are brand co-branding types, making it the most frequent form of cooperation.

      In addition to Lucky, a variety of new-style tea drinks are also keen to create co-branded models. Among them, on the day of the launch of the joint campaign between Bawang Chahime and Tomb Raiding Notes, on October 3, the sales volume of “Qingqing Nuoshan,” one of the joint products, increased by more than 130%. During the Mid-Autumn Festival and National Day, which coincided with the Asian Games, Shanghai Aunt Fresh Fruit Tea also took advantage of the sporting event boom. A new “Duo Golden Peach” series of drinks was launched, the champion e-contestant Yi Nuo was invited to serve as the “Fresh Fruit Tea Experience Officer,” and launched a variety of marketing activities such as joint marketing activities, customized gold medal sweepstakes, and drink voucher broadcasts.

      On September 14, Nai Xue's Tea Co-branded Thin Box, Fantasea Music Universe, launched a major co-branded milk tea. Orders for Nai Xue X Van Tersey's co-branded milk tea hit the moment it went on sale on the first day. The sales volume reached 1.46 million cups on the first day, setting a new record for the sales volume of Nai Xue's new products on the first day. At the same time, it also set a record for single-day store sales. While netizens all over the country are calling for Jay Chou's album “Memories Killing,” they also took the opportunity to launch “Drink Nai Xue Xian Milk Tea for 9.9 Yuan” and went viral.

      Earlier, in May of this year, Hi Cha also launched a co-branded product, and Fendi and Fendi launched a related package. At one point, the Heicha co-branded package, which costs 19 yuan per cup, was hyped up to hundreds of yuan.

      However, at the end of the day, consumers always pay attention to the product itself, and the core of a brand's survival is also the product itself. Co-branding is only a means to bring added value to the brand. Ultimately, consumers' long-term purchases still depend on product quality. Innovation in product development combined with the surprising experience brought by marketing can they have lasting appeal.
